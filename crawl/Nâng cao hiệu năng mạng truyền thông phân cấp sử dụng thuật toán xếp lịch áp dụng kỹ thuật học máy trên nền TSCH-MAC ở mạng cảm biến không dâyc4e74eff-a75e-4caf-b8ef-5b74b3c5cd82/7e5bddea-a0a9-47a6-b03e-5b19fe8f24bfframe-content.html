e) Kt lun
Lun vn gm hai phn l xy dng c s l thuyt v th nghim m phng:
C s l thuyt vi ba ci tin ln l thc hin a nh tuyn trn RPL, thc hin
truyn thng phn cp trn TSCH-MAC v xy dng thut ton xp lch TOML
p dng k thut hc my. Vi c ch a nh tuyn giao thc RPL truyn
thng CD s c dnh nhiu ti nguyn hn, c truyn trn ng truyn
ngn v khng phi tranh thng lng vi truyn thng RD c nh hng
ng truyn khc. Trong khi , vi truyn thng tch bit, xung t gia bn
tin CD v RD s b loi b khi cc khe thi gian ch chp nhn truyn thng d
liu mt instance c th v thi gian ch ca cc bn tin CD s thp hn RD khi
s khe thi gian chp nhn truyn thng d liu CD nhiu hn RD. Vi vic xy
dng thut ton xp lch TOML p dng k thut hc my trong bi ton phn
loi a lp nt mng gn cc tc v ngu nhin trn mt khe thi gian, sau
tnh ton t l thnh cng ca cc tc v v quyt nh tc v no l ti u trn
khe thi gian trong mt khung khe v gn tc v ti u trong sut thi gian
di, m bo mng hot ng tt nht trong phm vi c th ca thut ton xp
lch TOML.
Th nghim m phng trn h iu hnh Contiki v th vin m ngun contiki-
master. Th nghim so snh WSNs p dng thut ton xp lch TOML v phn
chia instance vi cc WSNs p dng cc cu hnh Orchestra trn nhiu mi
trng c kh truyn tin khc nhau. Kt qu th nghim chng minh tnh
ng n ca c s l thuyt: chnh lch hiu nng gia D liu Quan trng v
D liu Ph thng WSNs ci t thut ton xp lch TOML cao hn ng k so
