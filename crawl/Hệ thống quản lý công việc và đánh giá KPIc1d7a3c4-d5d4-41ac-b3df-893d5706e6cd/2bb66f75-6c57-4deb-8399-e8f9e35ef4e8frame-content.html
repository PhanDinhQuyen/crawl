Ni dung tm tt:
a) L do chn ti
Hin nay, mi mt cng ty hay t chc no cng cn phi c cc tiu ch
nh gi nng lc thc hin cng vic ca mi nhn vin v rt quan trng trong
qu trnh nh hng pht trin ca cng ty, t chc. Phng php nh gi hiu
qu cng vic c p dng ph bin l KPI (Key Performance Indicator).
KPI l mt h thng cc tiu ch nh gi v l mt cng c hu ch cho cc
nh qun l trong vic nh gi hiu qu cng vic ca tng nhn vin v ca
n v hay tp th. Qua nhng gi tr ca KPI, nh qun l c th thy c mc
hon thnh, mc ng gp ca nhn vin hay n v trong mt khong thi
gian nht nh v c th a ra cc quyt nh khen thng hay pht, khin trch
mt cch kp thi, cng bng, vn minh. KPI c rt nhiu thang o khc nhau, p
dng cho mi lnh vc khc nhau nhng khng phi thang o KPI no cng m
bo c tiu ch SMART (tiu ch bao gm: c th, o lng c, c minh
chng kt qu, gn vi cng vic thc th, c thi gian r rng) nn cc kt qu
KPI c th ch mang tnh nh tnh, thiu khch quan v cha phn nh c
ng thc trng cng vic ca mi c nhn.
Trong bi cnh xu hng lm vic t xa ang tr nn thnh hnh th cn
phi c mt c ch nh gi KPI hp l, c ghi nhn thng tin d liu v phn
nh c ng tnh trng thc t ca cng vic ca nhn vin. in hnh l i
dch SARS-CoV-2 nm 2020 va qua, khng t doanh nghip chao o v tnh

Thc t cng cho thy rng, khng t cc doanh nghip t chc s dng t
nht mt cng c qun l cng vic qun l cc cng vic ca n v, c nhn.
Vn t ra l phi qun l c cc cng vic, nh gi cc cng vic theo cc

Chng 2: M hnh v xut trong qun l cng vic v nh gi KPI:
Ni dung chng ny l xut s dng m hnh, c ch ca phng php
ng gng, phng php qun l chi ph, d bo nhn vin, xut tch hp d
liu t DXClan v web service cho bo co.
